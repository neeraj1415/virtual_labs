what is virtual lab?

The Virtual Laboratory is a computer-based learning environment where students are provided with an interactive environment for creating and conducting simulated experiments at their own pace and obtain confidence. Every experiment provides an introduction, procedure, demo, and simulation environment to perform the experiment, quizzes, report submission, and grading. With the help of the virtual labs, students nourish themselves with confidence in practical skills and a better understanding of the subject. In addition, these virtual labs prepare the students' confidence before they come to the laboratory to perform the experiment and manage the time effectively physically. Thus, the UG students, PG students, and Researchers of NITW can use these virtual labs to satisfy their thirst for knowledge in engineering and science at their own pace and their convenience.
